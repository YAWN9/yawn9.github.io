Curriculum Vitae

Born 23 November 1956 in Herrsching/Ammersee, Germany.
Lives and works in Edesheim, Rhineland-Palatinate.

1973 – 1976 Apprenticeship as process engraver in Kaiserslautern
1984 General University Entrance Qualification in Neuss
1986 Beginning of intensive involvement with the medium of photography
1989 First edition of the “Série Populaire”
1991 Agfa honorary prize of the “Large Format Photo Foundation”
Conception and construction of the “Landauer II” 8×10 inch enlarger
1994 Appointment to the “German Society of Photography” (DGPh)
1997 Degree in Psychology, University of Koblenz-Landau
1998 Foundation of the “Academia Palatina” to promote black-and-white photography
2004 Appointment to the “Joint Venture of Palatinate Artists” (APK)
2006 Landesmuseum Koblenz, Festung Ehrenbreitstein
